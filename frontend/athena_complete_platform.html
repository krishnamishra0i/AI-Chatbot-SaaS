Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Creative Reality Studio? ▼
The Creative Reality Studio is our powered platform that allows you to create AI-powered videos with photorealistic avatars. It's designed for content creators and enterprises.
Can I stream the generated video in real-time? ▼
Yes! Our API supports real-time streaming similar to live chat. You can integrate our technology into your applications for instantaneous avatar-based interactions.
What video formats and resolutions do you support? ▼
We support MP4, WebM, and HLS streaming with resolutions from 720p up to 4K with adaptive bitrate streaming for optimal performance.
How do I get an API key? ▼
Sign up for a developer account on our platform, navigate to the API section, and generate your API key instantly. Our documentation provides comprehensive integration guides.
What are the image upload requirements? ▼
We accept PNG and JPG formats up to 10MB. For best results, use clear, well-lit headshots. Minimum resolution 512x512, with 1024x1024 recommended.
What is the output video length? ▼
Studio videos can be generated from 15 seconds up to 10 minutes. For streaming and real-time interactions, the duration is unlimited.
